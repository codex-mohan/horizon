Built for power users

Everything you need, nothing you don't. Local-first by default, web-scale when you want it.

⚡

Local-First

Runs entirely on your machine. Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to deploy to the web.

🛠

System Tools

Execute shell commands, manage files, browse the web. The agent integrates with your actual operating system.

💬

Flat Conversations

No branching trees. No complex message grouping. Just a clean, linear chat that works like you think.

🎨

Multi-Model

Switch between OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, or local Ollama models on the fly. No lock-in, ever.

💻

Desktop Native

Built with Tauri. Fast, small footprint, native feel. Includes a CLI for power users who live in the terminal.

🌐

Web Deployable

Scale to the web with the Hono relay. Multi-user auth, PostgreSQL sessions, Prometheus metrics, Grafana dashboards.

Designed for focus

Solid surfaces. Sharp edges. No glassmorphism. Just clarity.
